Overview

This assessment does not require any personal data to be completed. The assessment itself runs in your browser; your in-progress answers are stored locally in your browser only.

What is stored when you submit results

If you choose to submit your results to the research study, the following is stored securely for research purposes:

  • The organization name you enter
  • An optional contact email (only if you provide one)
  • Your maturity answers and the resulting scores
  • A timestamp of the submission

Submitting is optional — you can complete the assessment without sending your results.
